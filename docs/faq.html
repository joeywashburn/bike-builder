Frequently Asked Questions

Installation

Where should I add the Bike Builder?

Create a dedicated page! Go to Online Store → Pages → Add page → "Build Your Bike". Then customize that page and add the Bike Builder block. This prevents the bike builder from slowing down your homepage or other pages.

How do I tag my products?

Tag products with bike-builder-frame, bike-builder-fork, etc. Enable/disable categories in Settings.

Performance

Will this slow down my site?

No, if used on a dedicated page. The bike builder loads only when customers visit that specific page. Do NOT add it to your homepage, header, or footer.

Customization

Can I customize colors and fonts?

Yes! Go to Settings → Theme Customization to change colors, fonts, header text, and more.
