Frequently Asked Questions

Setup & Installation

Where should I add the Bike Builder?

IMPORTANT: Create a dedicated page! Do not add to your homepage.

Why? The bike builder loads all your tagged products, which can slow down other pages. A dedicated page keeps your site fast.

How: Online Store → Pages → Add page → Title: "Build Your Dream Bike" → Customize → Add "Bike Builder" block → Save

How do I tag my products?

Tag each product with its category tag:

  • bike-builder-frame - Frames
  • bike-builder-fork - Forks
  • bike-builder-handlebar - Handlebars
  • bike-builder-stem - Stems
  • bike-builder-grips - Grips
  • bike-builder-brakes - Brakes
  • bike-builder-cranks - Cranks
  • bike-builder-pedals - Pedals
  • bike-builder-seat - Seats
  • bike-builder-wheel - Wheels/Wheelsets
  • And more! Enable/disable categories in Settings.

Do I need to sell every category?

No! Only enable the categories you actually stock. Go to Settings → Categories and uncheck what you don't sell.

Build Fee (Optional)

What is the build fee feature?

Charge customers for professional bike assembly. The fee automatically adds to cart when they build a bike.

How do I set up a build fee?

  1. Create a product: "Professional Bike Assembly" priced at your fee (e.g., $50)
  2. Copy the product ID from the URL
  3. Go to Bike Builder → Settings → Build Fee Configuration
  4. Enable build fee and paste the product ID
  5. Optionally: Set a threshold for free assembly (e.g., free on orders $500+)

Performance

Will this slow down my site?

No, if used on a dedicated page. The bike builder only loads when customers visit that specific page.

DO NOT add it to your homepage, header, footer, or product pages - this will slow down your entire site.

Customization

Can I customize colors and fonts?

Yes! Go to Settings → Theme Customization to change colors, fonts, header text, and button styles to match your brand.

Do product variants work?

Yes! Products with multiple variants (size, color, etc.) automatically show dropdown selectors. Customers pick their variant and the price updates.

Troubleshooting

Products aren't showing up

Check that:

  • Products are tagged correctly (e.g., bike-builder-frame)
  • Products are published and active
  • At least one variant is available for sale
  • The category is enabled in Settings

Add to Cart isn't working

Make sure all selected products have available inventory and are published to your online store.
