Installation Guide

Step 1: Install the App

  1. Visit the Shopify App Store
  2. Search for "Bike Builder"
  3. Click "Add app"
  4. Authorize the app (read_products permission)

Step 2: Tag Your Products

  1. Go to Products in your Shopify admin
  2. Edit each bike part product
  3. Add tags like bike-builder-frame, bike-builder-fork, etc.
  4. Save each product

Step 3: Configure Settings

  1. Open the Bike Builder app from your admin sidebar
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Enable the categories you want to show
  4. Customize colors, fonts, and text (optional)
  5. Set up build fee if desired (optional)
  6. Save settings

Step 4: Add to Your Theme

  1. Go to Online Store → Pages
  2. Click "Add page"
  3. Title: "Build Your Dream Bike"
  4. Save the page
  5. Go to Online Store → Themes → Customize
  6. Select your "Build Your Dream Bike" page from the dropdown
  7. Click "Add section"
  8. Find "Bike Builder" under Apps
  9. Add it to your page
  10. Save and publish

Step 5: Test

  1. Visit your bike builder page on the storefront
  2. Select some parts
  3. Verify the total updates correctly
  4. Click "Add to Cart"
  5. Complete a test checkout

Done!

Your bike builder is live! Add a link to your navigation menu so customers can find it.
