Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Bike Builder ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Shopify application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Store Information: Shop domain, store owner email, store name
  • Product Data: Product titles, tags, variants, and pricing (read-only access)
  • Merchant Settings: Theme customization preferences, enabled categories, build fee configuration
  • Session Data: Temporary session information for authentication

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Bike Builder service
  • Enable customers to build custom bikes on your storefront
  • Store merchant customization settings
  • Improve and optimize the app experience

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored securely on Railway infrastructure with encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • Regular security audits and updates are performed
  • Customer bike builds are session-based and not permanently stored

5. Data Sharing

  • We do NOT sell, trade, or share your data with third parties
  • Shopify has access to data as required by their platform
  • We only access data necessary to provide the bike builder functionality

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Merchant settings are retained while the app is installed
  • Upon app uninstallation, shop data is deleted within 48 hours
  • Customer data requests are fulfilled within 30 days per GDPR requirements
  • We comply with all GDPR, CCPA, and Shopify data protection requirements

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request data deletion
  • Export your data
  • Update or correct your information

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use session cookies for authentication purposes only. No third-party tracking or analytics cookies are used.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date and, for material changes, by providing notice through the app or email.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: joey@joeywashburn.com
  • App Support: Through the Bike Builder app in your Shopify admin
